## Ablation Failure

The full score is not robustly supported by its own ablations.

- Full score: `0.85034 +/- 0.04163` goal success.
- Minus irreversible-trap penalty: `0.86734 +/- 0.04055`.
- Minus failure probability: `0.86394 +/- 0.03333`.

These ablations improve success, so the objective is misspecified.

## Submission Readiness Blockers

- Local synthetic evidence only; no real robot, high-fidelity simulator, or accepted external benchmark.
- Strongest non-oracle baseline beats the proposed method on the main hard split.
- No decisive paired advantage over contingent replanning.
- Ablations contradict the intended recoverability decomposition.

## Revival Requirements

- Replace the synthetic benchmark with hardware or accepted high-fidelity benchmark evidence.
- Implement modern TAMP, contingent planning, and learned world-model baselines.
- Learn or calibrate the recoverability score from data.
- Show that recoverability reduces irreversible physical errors without hiding cost or damage tradeoffs.
